FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

* IS MACHINE HIRE AVAILABLE?

Unfortunately we are not able to offer you this option at this stage! However we will make sure we deliver the best quality of frozen yogurt to your event.

* FULL SERVICE AND DROP OFF?

Full service catering means our Noggians will stay at the event to help our Noggi lovers, making sure that they have the most awesome Noggi experience. Drop off service means you still have fun picking and choosing your favourite combination of flavours and toppings but just without the help of our Noggians!

* HOW MUCH IS IT TO HAVE NOGGI CATER?

The prices may vary depending on the type of service, location and also the event. Please fill in your details and send it to catering@noggi.com.au for more information!

* HOW DO YOU KEEP THE NOGGI COLD?

Noggi will be delivered to the designated area in a catering freezer which keeps it very cool for a long time!
