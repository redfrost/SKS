FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

* What is the estimated costs of setting up a Noggi Franchise?

Noggi Franchise Investment varies, depending on the size of the site, location, new or existing Noggi Franchise and individual circumstances.

Typical Noggi Set-up cost
$150,000 ~ $250,000 plus GST – Kiosk
$250,000 ~ $350,000 plus GST – Shop/Cafe
(excluding Franchisee Fee)

* How much is the Noggi Franchise fee?

Franchise Fee - $40,000 plus GST
Royalty – 8% of turnover
Marketing Fee – 2% of turnover

* Do I need to have a site already?

No, you are not required to have a site. Our Noggi Leasing Consultant will have a list of prime Noggi sites that have already been approved by the Head Office. However, we are more than happy to work alongside each other if you have secured a prime site already. We position Noggi in strategic locations based on comprehensive demographic research along with analysis of the surrounding outlets to enhance the quality café/kiosk experience that each fan deserves.

Location size: Kiosk 15sqm~30sqm, Shop 50sqm~70sqm, Café 80sqm~100sqm+

* Do I need franchise or hospitality experience?

Franchise or hospitality experience maybe useful, however no experience is required. Noggi has plans of expansion with business partners and franchisees along with the executive team that share a passion for our brand. Our operations team will train and guide you to become a successful Noggi Franchisee. We provide comprehensive training at our Sydney Head Office which will take approximately 2~4 weeks.

* How long will the Noggi Franchising process take?

Noggi Franchising process will usually take about 2~3 months minimum. It really depends on the applicants circumstances and how well both parties work with each other. This process involves franchise agreements, site leasing, shop design and fitting, training and recruitment.

* Do I have to work at the store?

Yes, we strongly recommend our Noggi Franchisees to be present at the store and perform all levels of management duties. A successful Noggi Franchisee should be able to show passion and commitment of the business.

* How is Noggi different to other Frozen Yogurt retailers?

We are absolutely passionate about High Quality and Taste. Our froyos and toppings are made from on the finest ingredients specially sourced from Italy, Japan, Asia and of course locally here in Australia along with our fresh fruits sourced from the local producers. We only use fresh milk and not powdered milk like some other froyo brands. Noggi’s special recipes have been perfected by our food specialists who ensures that our product swirls the perfect texture and tastes fresh and light every time.
